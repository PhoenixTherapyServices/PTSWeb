Sliding Scale
Sliding Scale agreements are developed between the client and the clinician. The clinician may ask for documentation to determine need. Sliding Scale agreements could be as low as $25 a session. Please contact us for more information.
Subscription Therapy
In March 2025 we launched subscription-style therapy. This allows clients to pay monthly for mental health services, and in most cases, it is less than a deductible or a fee for service agreement. This option also gives clients peace of mind knowing that their clinician is fully licensed and using evidence-based interventions, that are not always required with other subscription therapy services.

Disordered Eating Treatment Scholarship

Through the Britt Perrota Sommerfield Memorial Scholarship Fund, we provide low-cost to no-cost treatment for those struggling with eating disorders and trauma.
